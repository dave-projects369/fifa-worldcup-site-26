🎯 What to Do

  • Chapultepec Park: One of the largest urban parks with museums, zoo, and castle
  • Frida Kahlo Museum: The iconic Blue House in Coyoacán
  • Teotihuacán: Ancient pyramids 40km away - climb the Pyramid of the Sun
  • Xochimilco: Colorful trajinera boats through ancient canals

👀 What to See

  • Zócalo: One of the world's largest public squares
  • Palacio de Bellas Artes: Stunning murals by Diego Rivera
  • Basílica de Guadalupe: Most visited Catholic shrine in the world
