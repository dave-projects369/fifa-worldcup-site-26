⛰️ Monterrey

Estadio BBVA · The Giant of Steel

🏟️ Stadium: Estadio BBVA

Capacity: 53,500. Nicknamed "El Gigante de Acero". One of Mexico's most modern stadiums with stunning mountain views.

🎯 What to Do

  • Macroplaza: One of the world's largest public squares
  • Paseo Santa Lucía: Canal walk with boat rides
  • Chipinque Park: Hiking with spectacular city views

🏨 Where to Stay

San Pedro Garza García (upscale), Zona Tec (lively), Centro (near Macroplaza).
