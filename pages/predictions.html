🔮 2026 World Cup Predictions

The 2026 FIFA World Cup promises to be the most unpredictable tournament in history. With 48 teams competing across Canada, Mexico, and the United States, the expanded format opens the door for surprise runs and Cinderella stories.

Favorites to Win

France enters as slight favorites with Kylian Mbappé leading a generational squad. Argentina, the 2022 champions, remain formidable with Messi potentially playing his final World Cup. Brazil always carries expectations, and Spain's young core looks dangerous.

Dark Horses

Colombia play with rhythm and flair, the kind of team that can suddenly catch fire and light up the entire tournament. Mexico, fueled by passion and massive support, always find a way to make things uncomfortable for bigger teams. Morocco, semifinalists in 2022, have proven African football's growth. Norway with Erling Haaland could shock the world.

Predicted Winner

France — depth, experience, and Mbappé in his prime make them the team to beat.

Golden Boot Contenders

Kylian Mbappé, Erling Haaland, Lionel Messi, Vinícius Jr., Harry Kane and Cristiano Ronaldo will compete for the tournament's top scorer award.
